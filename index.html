Frequently Asked Questions

Our cake studio is open by appointment only for pick-ups and meetings. We do not have a retail store or any pre-made cakes for sale.

We are often booked weeks ahead of time, so the sooner you reserve your place on our calendar, the better. For special occasion cakes, we need at least a minimum of one week notice. For weddings, we ask for a minimum of three weeks notice. Please contact us to discuss our availability.

My cakes start at $4 a slice for weddings, but the overall, price varies depending on the details of the order. I price my cakes according to the price of ingredients, how much time it takes, and the level of difficulty.

We are so sorry, but we do not ship our cakes, they are too fragile! We will, however, hand deliver our cakes and treats! Delivery fees are calculated by the distance traveled to your event.

Yes. We offer tastings for wedding cake orders only, you will get 4 tastings for free!
